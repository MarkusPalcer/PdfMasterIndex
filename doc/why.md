# Why did I make this?

I have a bunch of rule books for each of the systems I am game-mastering and I wanted a quick way to find "Which rule is in which book?".
I know that OSes have index features, but I also wanted to just show which pages something is on, because oftentimes I have the physical book available and want to open it and hand it to the players.

That's why I made a quick tool which stores in a DB which word is on which pages of which document.
